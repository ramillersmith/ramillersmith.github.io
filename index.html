Transportation

We highly encourage walking and taking transit while you're in San Francisco! We have provided information on the closest transit stations to each venue below.
You can buy a Clipper Card from ticket machines at any major transit station for $3, or download on your phone via the Clipper app. If you have any questions, please let us know!

Transportation Costs:
  • BART from SFO to downtown San Francisco: $9.65
  • BART from Oakland Airport to downtown San Francisco: $11
  • BART within San Francisco: $2.10
  • Light Rail (), Streetcars (), and Buses: $2.50
  • Cable Cars: $8 per ride, or $13 for day pass on MuniMobile

    • Accommodations

    We recommend staying anywhere within walking distance of Market Street (with the exception of the area around Civic Center), or along The Embarcadero. The Market Street subway provides fast and frequent service through downtown and many other parts of San Francisco.

    Our dinner location is in the same building as the Hyatt Regency. Other cheap hotel options can often be found in the vicinity of Union Square. If you have questions about staying in particular neighborhoods, please let us know!

    Dress Code

    The wedding will be very small and casual, so feel free to wear whatever you'd like. We do recommend wearing comfortable shoes as we will likely be moving between multiple locations!

    Dinner

    There is no need to select a meal option. However, if you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know!

    Covid-19

    Also be aware that as of now, many San Francisco restaurants still require proof of vaccination. Please be prepared to bring your Covid-19 vaccine card (or a picture of it) with you to San Francisco!
